The Permanent residency program offers non-EU nationals a guaranteed permanent residency with a single real estate investment of €300,000. The permanent residency program applies to the investor, their spouse and children up to the age of 25 years old.

The permits are valid forever and the process is straightforward

The Permanent residency program

A minimum investment of €300,000 plus VAT in a residential property

Permanent residency is granted for life. No renewal is required

Permanent residency applies to all the family including children under the age of 18 years old as well as children up to the age of 25 years old, if financially dependent

Upon receipt of permanent residency, the applicant is required to visit Cyprus once within the first year and then every two (2) years

No language requirements

Benefits

A risk free investment in real estate property of €300,000 plus VAT

Residence permit is valid for life

Travel within Europe with ease

Enables you to own a business in Cyprus

The right to apply for Cypriot Citizenship

Permanent residency is granted within two (2) months from submission of application

No inheritance tax

Access to a first class health system and education
