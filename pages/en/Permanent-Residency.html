Obtain residency in Cyprus for you and your family through the Cyprus government Fast-Track Procedure

Buying property in Cyprus is the best and fastest way to obtain a permanent residency permit with terms which are unsurpassed in Europe.

Benefits of obtaining a permanent residence permit on Cyprus

  • Issued within 2 – 3 months
  • Life-long validity without renewal
  • Family members can be included in the application (spouse, dependent children up to the age of 25 years old and parents)
  • No requirement to reside in Cyprus, however, applicants must visit the country at least once every 2 years to maintain the permit. Other European countries require the applicant to reside in the country for 3 months of each year
  • The possibility to have a business in Cyprus and to receive shareholder dividends

Terms and Conditions

  • The purchase of a NEW PROPERTY or 2 NEW PROPERTIES from the same developer with a total value of €300,000, excluding V.A.T.
  • At the same time of the submission of the application €200,000 (excluding V.A.T.) must be transferred in settlement of the property and the contract of sale must be registered with the Land Registry Office
  • Funds transferred from abroad and deposited in a local Cypriot bank for the amount of €30,000 for a period of 3 years
  • A secured annual income from abroad and from sources other than employment in Cyprus
  • The residence permit holder must visit Cyprus at least once every two years to maintain his/her status
  • A clean criminal record, the applicant, his/her spouse and children over the age of 16 should provide a clean criminal record from the country of residence which should be certified

Additional Benefits

  • No language requirements
  • Lower tuition fees for non-EU Cypriot permanent residence permit holders, as they are considered permanent residents of an EU country Cyprus is a common law jurisdiction
  • Property acquired is freehold
  • No inheritance tax
  • Low property tax
  • Low crime rate in Europe
