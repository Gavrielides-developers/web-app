This unique piece of land consists of 12 separate Title Deeds totaling 8637m². The land is free of any mortgages or other burdens

Location – Kato Paphos

Located in an elevated position at the top of the tourist area offering views over the roof tops to the sea (South to Southwest).

It is 400m from the new Eco City called ”Neopolis” and 300m from a private hospital.

It is easily accessed from all 4 directions and nothing will be built behind it because of an already existing municipal park on its North side.

Use of Land

This land is suitable for residential use, but offices, clinics or other commercial buildings can also be allowed by the Town Planning Authorities.

The topography of the land allows for imaginative designs of a proposed development of apartments and various other facilities.

State of Land

* Undeveloped

* Building ratio is 80% (Because of Town Planning Regulations with exemptions of balconies and corridors etc., this figure can rise to 90%).

* Maximum height is 4 floors
