New Project

New release for Rentals

A modern 4-floor building consisting of six apartments and one penthouse

Project

Situated on Z. Papantoniou Street, Ayioi Omologites, Nicosia, the building is near the Hilton Hotel and next to the Ministry of Finance. Close to all amenities such as markets, universities, schools, etc.

Completion: September 2020

Features

  • A large (5x11m) swimming pool
  • Uncrowded sunbathing
  • Changing Rooms/W.C. available next to pool
  • Covered parking
  • Storage Rooms
  • Covered Verandas
  • Five minutes’ walk to all amenities, banks, restaurants, shops, pubs, etc.
  • Few minutes’ walk to beach and the harbour
  • All windows and patio doors are double glazed
  • Pressurised water system
  • Solar heating
  • Provision for air condition
  • Gated access
  • Situated in a convenient location between Tombs of the Kings area and the Municipal Market of Paphos
