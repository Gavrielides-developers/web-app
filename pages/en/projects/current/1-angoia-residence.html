New Project

Anogia Residence

A carefully designed project of luxurious apartments in an excellent location

Project

A four storey building designed to allow views and privacy and stands out from surrounding buildings thanks to its unique design.

The residential building is sited in the Moutallos area, close to the Tombs of the Kings landmark.

Features

  • A large (5x11m) swimming pool
  • Uncrowded sunbathing
  • Changing Rooms/W.C. available next to pool
  • Covered parking
  • Storage Rooms
  • Covered Verandas
  • Five minutes’ walk to all amenities, banks, restaurants, shops, pubs, etc.
  • Few minutes’ walk to beach and the harbour
  • All windows and patio doors are double glazed
  • Pressurised water system
  • Solar heating
  • Provision for air condition
  • Gated access
  • Situated in a convenient location between Tombs of the Kings area and the Municipal Market of Paphos

The four storey building provides six two-bedroom apartments on the first two floors each with covered verandas. The third floor offers two two-bedroom apartments both with covered verandas.

The fourth floor is occupied by a three-bedroom penthouse with private access to the roof garden, which includes a BBQ and sitting area and an outdoor jacuzzi.

Storage units and secure parking are provided for all residents. The common swimming pool area with ample sunbathing area provides privacy and is surrounded by flowers and shrubs.

The building is designed to allow views and privacy and stands out from surrounding buildings thanks to its unique design.
