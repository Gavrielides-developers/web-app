Tala Village

The village Tala is found in the district of Paphos, at a distance of 8 kilometers north of the city of Paphos, within a magical natural environment, famous for its cool breezes and boasting spectacular views to the Mediterranean Sea. This traditional Cypriot village is surrounded by paved pedestrian areas, traditional tavernas, and local coffee shops. A recently constructed amphitheater and park host a series of cultural events throughout the year.

Other amenities such as supermarkets, shops and banks are all within walking distance. Just a five minute drive away is the championship golf course at Tsada , and with the Aphrodite Hills and Secret Valley golf course being only 25 minutes away, the Tala area is rich for golf lovers.
