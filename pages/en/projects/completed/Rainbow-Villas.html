Project Features

Situated to the south of this small residential village, set high on a hill in a stunning location, the Rainbow Villas development benefits from panoramic views; lush, green surroundings; and close proximity to Tsada’s 18-hole championship golf course.

Although a haven of peace and tranquility, Tsada has all the usual amenities and is well connected and easily accessible by main road. The village lies just 5km northwest of Paphos centre, 6km northeast from the beatiful beaches of Coral Bay and Corallia, and the airport is just a 25 minute drive away.

This prestigious development of nine detached 3 and 4-bedroomed properties has been finished to extremely high specifications and include many features as standard which are other companies’ extras. It has also been meticulously designed and positioned to ensure unobstructed views across the scenic hills and valleys to the sea and coastline below.
