Project Features

Sirena Sunrise is situated in a quiet setting just 7 minutes walk from The Sea Front and 5 minutes walk to the centre of Kato Paphos with itâ€™s restaurants, bars, banks and shops.

The large swimming pool is the focal point with features such as an ornamental bridge and small islands with palm trees and colourful shrubs (now supplemented by a riot of colour from the flowering trellises of the new occupants). Surrounding the pool is an 800m2 patio for leisurely uncrowded sun-bathing.

A selection of 1,2&3 bedroom units of apartments and houses, well designed internally and carefully positioned around the pool to give maximum space and privacy.
