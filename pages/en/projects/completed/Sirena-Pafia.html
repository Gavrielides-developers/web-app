Project Features

Sirena Pafia is a project of only nine apartments, with a private swimming pool & very attractive gardens. Situated in a select residential area of kato Paphos, edged by parks and only 3 minutes walk away from the sea front and tourist area with all its amenities.

Continuing on the success of our previous projects, Sirena Kings, Sirena Riverside, Sirena Sunrise, Sirena George, Sirena Cypria and Sirena Olympia.
