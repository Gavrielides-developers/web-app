Coral Bay

This area is considered as being the jewel of the western coast of the island because of the crystal water and sandy beaches, the richness in variety of flora and fauna, its proximity to the Akamas area known for its Mediterranean tranquility, and being one of the few remaining breeding places for the green Turtle.
