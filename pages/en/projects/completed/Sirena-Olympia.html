Project Features

Set in a quiet, elevated position 15 minutes walk to the sea front, with sea views from the upper floors, this complex is located in a much sought after area of Kato Paphos. The natural cliffs to the rear provide a very peaceful and uncluttered setting and ensure against future building work directly overlooking the complex.

Two large swimming pools provide the focal point of the development, with features such as ornamental bridges, palm trees and colourful shrubs. The surrounding natural stone patios cover 800m2 to allow for leisurely sunbathing.

The apartments comprise a lounge / dining area, a master bedroom with ensuite bathroom, a second bathroom, a single bedroom, an open plan fitted kitchen and they are fully furnished and air-conditioned.
