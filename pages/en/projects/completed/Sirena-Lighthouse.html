Project Features

Sirena Lighthouse is an exclusive gated complex of 17 apartments with a kidney shaped swimming pool with sunbathing area lying just off the Tombs of the Kings road situated 250m from the sea and within easy walking distance of the Kings Avenue Mall, restaurants, banks, shops and close to public transport.
