Project Features

Sirena Riverside is a unique project of only seven,1 and 2 bedroom apartments, with a private swimming pool & very attractive gardens. Situated in a select residential area of kato Paphos, edged by parks and only 3 minutes walk away from the sea front and tourist area with all its amenities.
